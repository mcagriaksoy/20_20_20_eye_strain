Reduce Eye Strain with the 20-20-20 Rule
Spending a lot of time in front of a screen? Try this simple trick to keep your eyes feeling fresh:

Every 20 minutes, take a break from your screen.
Look at something 20 feet (6 meters) away for at least 20 seconds.
